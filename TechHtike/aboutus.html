Company Profile


U Myat Soe Win stands as a distinguished entrepreneur and one of Myanmar's most renowned electronic and IT retailers. A dedicated family man, he is the visionary founder and Managing Director of Technoland Computer Trading Company Limited.

In March 2000, U Myat Soe Win embarked on his entrepreneurial journey by opening the first Technoland store in Yangon, Myanmar. With his exceptional leadership and unwavering commitment, Technoland flourished over the next 17 years, expanding into a network of 14 stores, complete with showrooms and service centers, spread across Myanmar.

In 2022, U Myat Soe Win took on a new venture, launching the Sandy Bay Resort on the picturesque Ngwe Saung Beach, further showcasing his versatile entrepreneurial spirit.
